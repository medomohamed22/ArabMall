Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Donate Way ("we", "us", or "our") operates the donation platform accessible at https://arabmall.netlify.app (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Site to make donations using Pi Network cryptocurrency.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This policy applies solely to information collected through the Site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Pi Network Username: When you authenticate via Pi Browser, we receive your public Pi Network username for display purposes only.
  • Donation Details: Transaction amount, selected campaign, timestamp, transaction ID (txid), and payment ID provided by Pi Network after a successful donation.
  • Technical Data: Automatically collected data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and access times (standard server logs provided by Netlify).

We do not collect or store any private keys, wallet addresses, or sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Process and record your donations securely via the Pi Network blockchain.
  • Display your username on the Site to personalize your experience (e.g., "Welcome, [username]").
  • Maintain records of donations for transparency and internal reporting.
  • Improve the Site's functionality and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations if required.

3. Pi Network Integration

All payments are processed directly through the official Pi Network SDK and blockchain. We do not have access to your Pi wallet private keys or funds. Transactions are completed on the Pi blockchain and are irreversible once confirmed.

4. Data Storage and Security

Donation records are stored securely using serverless database services (such as Fauna or similar providers). We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS), secret environment variables, and access controls.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain donation records indefinitely for transparency and historical purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us (see section 9).

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Site does not use cookies or any third-party tracking technologies (e.g., Google Analytics). The only data collection occurs during Pi authentication and donation processing.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us
